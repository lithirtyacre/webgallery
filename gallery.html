I had a lot of fun making these because while I had to make a specific type of photoshop art I still had creative freedom in what was in it. The first picture I called creature. The objective was simple; fuse 2-3 images together to make a creature. What I made of it is an energetic and creepy picture. The second picture I called Bite Of The World for obvious reasons. We used masking techniques and morph tools for this project. The third picture I called Ghost Eagle. I made that one for fun and messed around with the filters to give it a transparent ghosty vibe. The fourth picture I called Chromatic Corridor. This piece was inspired by Fauvism art, poping colors and what not. In all my art I have to be careful to make perfect selections so the pictures dont look choppy, I also have to make sure to use pictures with great resolution so Ms. Freedman wont give me an F.
